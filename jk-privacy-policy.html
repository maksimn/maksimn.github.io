Jackknife Game App Privacy Policy.

The Jackknife game app does not contain its own program logic that is responsible for storing or collecting any personal data.

Instead, the Jackknife game app includes third-party components shipped by Google (c) that can use your personal data to show personalized ads. Your personal data will be processed and information from your device (cookies, unique identifiers, and other device data) may be stored by, accessed by and shared with third party vendors. Some vendors may process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interest.
