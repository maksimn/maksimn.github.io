V-knife App Privacy Policy.

V-knife app does not contain its own program logic that is responsible for storing or collecting any personal data.

Instead, the V-knife app includes third-party components shipped by Google that can use your personal data to show personalized ads. Your personal data will be processed and information from your device (cookies, unique identifiers, and other device data) may be stored by, accessed by and shared with third party vendors. Some vendors may process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interest.
